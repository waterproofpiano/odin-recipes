Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter
  • A plate, dummy

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on top of the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Stack slices together with the condiments facing inwards
  4. Put on a plate and enjoy
