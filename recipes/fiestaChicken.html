Fiesta Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 thing of frozen chicken
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of black beans, rinsed
  • 1 can of corn (or one cup of frozen corn)
  • taco seasoning
  • lime juice
  • tajin
  • 1 cup uncooked rice

Steps

  1. Combine frozne chicken, diced tomatoes, corn and seasoning (to taste).
  2. Rinse and add black beans.
  3. Stir and cook.
    • For instant pot, add 1 cup of vegetable stock, chicken stock, or water and cook for 40 minutes.
    • For slow cooker, cook for 8 hours for tender chicken.
  4. 20 minutes prior to serving, cook rice with a 2:1 ratio of water to rice.
  5. Enjoy!
